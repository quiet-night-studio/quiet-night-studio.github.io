Projects

Sokodot

A remake of Sokodot using the Godot game engine

 

This is v1.0.0 of our first project, a Sokoban game named “Sokodot”. It was developed & born using the Godot game engine, and multiple days of reading the Godot documentation/watching tutorials.


The gameplay is simple, move your little person using the WASD or Arrow keys, and to win the level you push the crate/box to the “spot” to proceed.


There isn’t a lot to see on the surface, but both of us gained a massive amount of knowledge on how to use this game engine and the basics of GDScript.

Roadmap

There will be a song playing. Versions can be played on both Mobile and PC platforms. Tiles will start to fall from the top of the screen and the player has to press the correct keys at the right time.

  • find songs that can be played in the game
    • royalty free songs.
  • program the tiles to fall on the correct order and time
    • it should be based on the precision of the click (fair, perfect...)
  • check when the player press one of the keys
    • accept multiple keys pressed at the same time.
  • ranking system
    • song score based on accuracy.
    • track combo?
  • player score per song (individual, global)
  • level editor
  • make our own songs
  • reward system
